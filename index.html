FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Luna

Is Luna completely free to use? +

Yes! Luna offers a free-tier plan that will give you the simple features, however, there are also paid-tier plans that you can buy for affordable price!

How do I set up my first reminder? +

Simply click the "Plus" button on the dashboard and create a new reminder with a title, description and time deadline that you must meet!

Can I use Luna on multiple devices? +

Absolutely! Since Luna is web-based as of now, if you log in into your account on phone, the reminders that you created on your PC will sync to your phone!

What makes Luna different from other reminder apps? +

Luna combines clean and polished User Interface and makes reminding simple and beginner-friendly. This makes very Luna enjoyable to use.

How secure is my data with Luna? +

We take data security seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I collaborate with others using Luna? +

Not yet! However, we plan to release this function soon.
